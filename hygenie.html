Change regularly

Change pads/tampons every 4–8 hours (or sooner for heavy flow).

Wash hands

Before and after changing products. Use sanitizer if water isn’t available.

Prevent rashes

Keep skin dry; consider a thin layer of petroleum jelly if changing is delayed.

Breathable underwear

Cotton helps airflow and reduces irritation.

Proper disposal

Wrap used products and place in trash. Do not flush.

On the go

Pack spares, a small bag for discreet disposal, and an extra pair of underwear.
