Hygiene Tips

Good hygiene during your period keeps you comfortable and healthy.

Change Products Regularly

Pads and tampons should be changed every 3–6 hours to avoid odor and infection.

Wash with Water

Clean your genital area with plain water, avoid harsh soaps.

Carry Extras

Keep spare pads or tampons in your bag just in case your period starts unexpectedly.
