Q&A with Dr. Vani Venkatachalam

Q: How often should I change my pad?

A: Every 4–6 hours to prevent odor and infection.

Q: Can I exercise during my period?

A: Yes! Exercise can help reduce cramps and boost mood.

Q: Are irregular periods normal?

A: They can be, especially in the first few years after starting your period. If you’re concerned, check with a doctor.
