Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water.
  2. Remove the pad from its wrapper and unfold it completely.
  3. Peel off the backing strip to expose the adhesive.
  4. Center the pad in your underwear with the adhesive side down.
  5. Press down firmly to help it stick properly.
  6. If the pad has wings, wrap them around the underwear.
