The Reproductive System

The menstrual cycle is part of the female reproductive system. It involves the uterus, ovaries, fallopian tubes, and vagina. Every month, the body prepares for a possible pregnancy, and if it doesn’t happen, the uterus sheds its lining — that’s your period.
