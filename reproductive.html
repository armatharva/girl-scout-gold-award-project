The Female Reproductive System

Understanding your body helps you feel more confident and prepared.

Uterus

The organ where a fertilized egg can grow. During periods, the lining sheds if there’s no pregnancy.

Ovaries

Release eggs each month and produce hormones like estrogen and progesterone.

Fallopian Tubes

Pathways that connect the ovaries to the uterus. Fertilization usually happens here.

Vagina

The passage leading from the uterus to outside the body. Menstrual blood flows through here.
