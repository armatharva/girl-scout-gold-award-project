Myth: You can’t play sports on your period.

Fact: You can. Exercise may reduce cramps and boost mood.

Myth: Period blood is “dirty.”

Fact: It’s a mix of blood and uterine lining — a healthy, normal process.

Myth: You shouldn’t bathe during your period.

Fact: Bathing and gentle washing help hygiene and comfort.

Myth: All cycles are 28 days.

Fact: “Normal” varies widely. Many healthy cycles aren’t exactly 28 days.

Myth: Tampons always cause TSS.

Fact: TSS is rare. Change tampons every 4–8 hours and use the lowest absorbency needed.

Myth: You can’t swim on your period.

Fact: You can swim using a tampon or menstrual cup.
