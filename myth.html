Myths & Facts

Myth: You can’t swim on your period.

Fact: You can swim while using tampons or menstrual cups.

Myth: Period blood is dirty.

Fact: It’s just blood and tissue from the uterus — completely natural.

Myth: Having your period means you’re sick.

Fact: Periods are a healthy sign your body is working normally.
