Myths & Facts

Bust the myths. Know the facts. Feel confident.

Myth: You can’t play sports.

Fact: You can. Exercise may reduce cramps and boost mood.

Myth: Period blood is “dirty.”

Fact: It’s a healthy shedding of the uterine lining.

Myth: You shouldn’t bathe.

Fact: Bathing or showering helps comfort and hygiene.

Myth: All cycles are 28 days.

Fact: Normal ranges vary widely person to person.

Myth: Tampons always cause TSS.

Fact: TSS is rare. Use the lowest absorbency and change every 4–8 hours.

Myth: You can’t swim on your period.

Fact: You can with a tampon or menstrual cup.
