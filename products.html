Period Products

Pads

Easy to use, widely available, but produce more waste.

Tampons

Discreet, can swim with them, but risk of TSS if not changed regularly.

Menstrual Cups

Reusable, eco-friendly, lasts up to 10 years, but learning curve is higher.

Period Underwear

Comfortable, washable, great backup, but expensive upfront.
