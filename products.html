Choosing the Right Product

Everyone’s body and flow are different. Try what feels comfortable and safe for you.

Product Pros Cons
Pads Easy to use; widely available; no internal insertion. Need changing every 4–8 hours; can feel bulky during sports.
Tampons Great for swimming/sports; discreet. Risk of TSS if worn too long; insertion may take practice.
Menstrual Cups Reusable; eco-friendly; cost-effective over time. Learning curve for insertion; must be cleaned/sterilized.
Period Underwear Comfortable; reusable; eco-friendly. Higher upfront cost; requires washing/drying.

Cost tips

Buying in bulk, reusable options (cups/underwear), and checking school/community programs can reduce costs.

Eco notes

Reusable options reduce waste; dispose of single-use products in trash (don’t flush).

Availability

Most pharmacies, supermarkets, schools, clinics, and community centers stock or offer free supplies.
