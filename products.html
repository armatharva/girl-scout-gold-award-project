Menstrual Products

Compare pads, tampons, cups, and period underwear — learn the pros, cons, cost, and eco-friendliness.

Pads

Easy to use, disposable, available everywhere. Must be changed every 3–4 hours.

Tampons

Inserted inside the vagina, more discreet. Change every 4–6 hours.

Menstrual Cups

Reusable, eco-friendly, lasts years. Requires comfort with insertion.

Period Underwear

Washable, eco-friendly. Can be used alone or as backup.
